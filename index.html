About Dhupali Homestay Samse

Locally owned and operated homestay dedicated to providing travelers with a home away from home! Located beside the sacred Sri 1008 Bhagawan Shantinatha Digambara temple in Samse, Kalasa, we offer an authentic and memorable experience in the heart of nature.

Nature: Experience our serene retreat nestled amidst lush greenery! Immerse yourself in the lap of nature, surrounded by picturesque landscapes and the melodious chirping of birds. It's the perfect escape from city life.

Culinary Delights: Indulge your taste buds in a culinary journey with our freshly prepared meals. From local delicacies to comfort food, our menu (Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner) is designed to tantalize your senses and leave you craving for more.

Adventure & Tours: Embark on thrilling trekking and captivating sightseeing tours, all conveniently located near our homestay. Our expert guides will accompany you on exciting trails, unveiling hidden gems, breathtaking viewpoints, and cascading waterfalls of the region.
