Why Choose Dhupali Homestay Samse?

Discover Karnataka's best-kept secret at our locally owned homestay in Samse, Kalasa. Strategically located beside the sacred Sri 1008 Bhagawan Shantinatha Digambara temple, we offer an authentic Western Ghats experience that combines traditional hospitality with modern comfort.

Prime Location & Nature: Nestled in the pristine Western Ghats, our homestay offers breathtaking views of lush green landscapes. Wake up to melodious bird songs and experience the tranquility that only nature can provide. Perfect for digital detox and rejuvenation.

Authentic Karnataka Cuisine: Savor traditional Karnataka delicacies prepared with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Our home-cooked meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner) showcase authentic flavors of the region, including specialties like Akki Rotti, Neer Dosa, and regional curries.

Expert-Guided Adventures: Explore the Western Ghats with our experienced local guides. Trek to Netravati Peak (highest peak in Karnataka), visit the famous Hornadu Annapoorneshwari Temple, discover hidden waterfalls like Soormane Falls and Yelaneeru Falls, and explore Kudremukh National Park. We also arrange visits to Kalasa, Kurinjal, and other scenic destinations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reach Dhupali Homestay Samse from Bangalore? +
From Bangalore, drive via Hassan-Alur-Kalasa route (approximately 280 km, 5-6 hours). Alternatively, take a bus to Kalasa and then local transport to Samse. We can arrange pickup from Kalasa bus stand for a small fee.
What trekking and sightseeing options are available? +
We organize guided treks to Netravati Peak (highest in Karnataka), Kudremukh, and visits to Soormane Falls, Yelaneeru Falls, Hornadu Temple, and Kurinjal. Our experienced local guides ensure safe and memorable experiences. Trek packages include guide, meals, and basic equipment.
What meals are included and what type of cuisine do you serve? +
We serve authentic Karnataka cuisine including breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Specialties include Akki Rotti, Neer Dosa, traditional curries, and local vegetables. We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, and special dietary requirements with advance notice.
What are your check-in/check-out times and cancellation policy? +
Check-in: 12:00 PM, Check-out: 11:00 AM. We're flexible with timings based on availability. Cancellation: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before arrival. For group bookings and extended stays, we offer customized policies.
Do you provide Wi-Fi and what amenities are available? +
Yes, we provide complimentary Wi-Fi, 24/7 hot water, attached bathrooms, room service, laundry facilities, and parking. Common areas include a library, games area, and terrace with mountain views. We also assist with local transportation arrangements.
